## Known history (pre-engine)
- 3,200 cold emails -> 0.09% CTR. Mass generic email to local businesses: DEAD channel.
- Meta ads: $1,100, 12,420 LP views at $0.09, 0 conversions. Cheap clicks = junk clicks. Paid social for $500/mo B2B: DEAD at this stage.
- Google Ads: conversion tag was unverified — data unusable. If ads resume later, verify tracking FIRST.
- 29 self-serve signups prove demand exists when people find the product.
  Activation is the bottleneck, not interest.

## July 6-22: What the sprint taught us

### Channels DEAD
- Cold calling US businesses: 30+ dials, near-zero pickups. Nobody answers unknown numbers.
- Cold texting: 20+ leads + all trials. Zero responses.
- AI rescue calls: 13 calls, 2 human connects, 0 conversions.
- Mass re-engagement emails: 21 sent, 0 replies.

### Channels ALIVE  
- Google Ads search: 274 clicks. People ARE searching. Demand exists.
- Demo line: 2 strangers called from ads (but heard wrong greeting — FIXED)
- Inbound self-serve: only path that produced real interest. Optimize this.

### Critical discovery (Jul 22)
- Market is CROWDED: Upfirst $25, Rosie $49, AskBenny $99, Smith.ai, Goodcall
- Generic positioning = price comparison we lose
- PIVOT: "The AI receptionist that shows you the money"

### Conversion tag was broken
- Fired on test/direct access URLs → false "5 conversions" in Google Ads
- FIXED: only fires after real Stripe payment. All prior data unusable.

## Objections heard
- "Not interested" (x3) — said before hearing pitch. Cold call rejection, not product rejection.
- Implicit: "$199 vs $25 competitors" — lives in the search results
