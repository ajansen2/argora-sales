# ARGORA VOICE — 90-DAY CUSTOMER ACQUISITION PLAYBOOK

Goal: **10 paying customers by October 6, 2026.** At $500/mo retail (or mixed
with $200/mo partner seats), that's $3,000–5,000 MRR and proof the business works.

The strategy in one line: founder-led selling into two verticals, powered by
the warmest asset we own (24 dormant trials), with a partner channel layered on
top. No paid ads until there's a repeatable manual sale.

---

## Week 1–2: Harvest what already exists
1. Export the 29 signups from Supabase into pipeline.csv.
2. Call the 3 active users. Learn why they stay, what it's worth to them,
   and ask each for one referral and permission for a case study.
3. Run /trial-rescue on all 24 dormant trials. Target: 10 setup calls booked.
4. Set the EARLY_ADOPTER_MODE end date publicly (suggest: Aug 1). Grandfathered
   rate for anyone active before then. A free product with no deadline converts nobody.
5. Set up the self-demo line — a phone number anyone can call to talk to the AI.
   This is the whole sales motion's centerpiece.

## Week 3–6: The manual machine (repeat weekly)
Adam's weekly quota — non-negotiable, tracked in /daily:
- 25 cold calls (5/day, 7:00–8:30am for trades, 2–4pm for clinics)
- 10 personalized emails/DMs (drafted by /outreach, sent by Adam)
- 2 partner conversations (agencies first — /partner)
- Every follow-up due that day, done that day
Local advantage: start Edmonton/Calgary/Red Deer. "I'm local, I'll drive over
and set it up" beats every SaaS competitor's onboarding flow.

## Week 7–12: Double down on whatever converted
By week 6 the data will show which of the four paths produced customers:
trial rescue, cold calls, partners, or referrals. Kill the weakest, double the
strongest. Only after 10 manual sales does paid acquisition get revisited —
and then Google Search only (high-intent keywords like "answering service for
HVAC"), with verified conversion tracking, never Meta.

## The channels, ranked by expected yield
1. **Trial rescue** — already interested, zero acquisition cost.
2. **Referrals from the 3 actives** — offer a free month per referral.
3. **Partner/agency channel** — slowest to start, biggest ceiling.
4. **Local cold calls with the demo-line close** — the product sells itself
   if we can get it into their ear for 30 seconds.
5. **Google Business Profile + micro case studies** — passive credibility so
   prospects who google "Argora" find proof, not silence.

## What is explicitly OFF the table for 90 days
- Meta ads, display ads, mass cold email blasts
- New products, new features (except bugs blocking a paying customer)
- FITR / Global Bites during work hours (evenings, capped)
- Any project on the frozen list in CLAUDE.md

## Scoreboard definitions (what /daily reports)
- MRR and paying-customer count (the only numbers that matter)
- Activation rate: trials contacted → setup calls → live on their phone line
- Call volume vs. 25/week quota
- Demos booked, partner meetings held
- Days since last new customer (the anti-complacency number)
