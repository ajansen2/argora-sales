# Partner Channel

The math: a partner pays $200/client/mo and resells at $500–800. One agency
with 5 clients = $1,000/mo recurring to Argora from a single relationship.
Partners are worth 5–10x the outreach effort of an end customer.

## Who to target (in priority order)
1. **Local-service marketing agencies** — they run Google Ads for HVAC/plumbers/
   dentists. Their nightmare: driving paid calls that the client never answers.
   Argora Voice makes *their* ads convert. This is the strongest pitch we have.
2. **MSPs / IT providers** serving clinics and trades — they already sell the
   phone system; this is an attach.
3. **VoIP/phone-system resellers** (RingCentral/Twilio ecosystem shops).
4. **Bookkeepers/business consultants** for trades (lighter fit, referral fee
   instead of reseller margin).

## Research process (like /leads but for partners)
Find agencies whose own websites name our verticals ("marketing for HVAC",
"dental marketing"). Capture: agency, principal's name, city, client evidence
(case studies on their site = the hook), fit score. Append to pipeline.csv
with segment=PARTNER.

## The partner pitch (draft per target)
- Email under 120 words. Core line: "You're spending your client's budget
  making phones ring that nobody answers. I'll show you the missed-call report
  for one of your clients — if it's zero, I'll leave you alone."
- Offer: white-label option, $200/client/mo cost, they set retail, first
  client free for 30 days, we handle setup.
- CTA: 20-minute call + live demo number.
