# Content Engine (sales-support mode)

Content exists to warm up the two verticals and give Adam credibility when a
prospect googles him. It is NOT a growth channel by itself at this stage.
Cap: 3 pieces per week, max 30 minutes of Adam's time each.

## Content types (in order of value)
1. **Micro case study** — the moment a real user gets a real result ("An
   Edmonton HVAC company's AI receptionist booked a furnace repair at 11pm
   Tuesday"). Requires a true event from learnings.md. These become sales
   ammunition, landing page proof, and posts simultaneously.
2. **Missed-call math posts** — vertical-specific: "What a missed call costs
   an HVAC company" with honest, sourced numbers.
3. **Build-in-public founder posts** — Adam's real numbers and lessons. Honest
   beats polished; include real setbacks.
4. **Landing page CRO** — propose concrete copy changes to the Argora Voice
   site: vertical-specific headlines, demo phone number above the fold,
   the grandfathered-pricing deadline.

## Rules
- Every piece must be true and traceable to something in learnings.md or
  activity-log.md. No invented customers, no invented numbers.
- Every piece ends with the demo number CTA where format allows.
- Voice: plain, specific, tradesperson-respectful. Ban the CLAUDE.md banned words.
- LinkedIn posts: under 150 words, first line must work as a hook on its own.
- After drafting, log in activity-log.md with intended publish date.

## Do not
- Do not start a blog, YouTube channel, or multi-platform calendar. If Adam
  asks for one, remind him of the focus rule and confirm he wants to override.
