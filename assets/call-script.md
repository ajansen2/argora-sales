OPENER (15s): "Hi [name], it's Adam with Argora here in Edmonton. Quick one —
I noticed [SPECIFIC HOOK]. I run a phone answering service built for [trade].
It picks up the calls you're missing, books the job, texts the customer back.
Got 60 seconds or should I call back after your morning jobs?"

DISCOVERY (then be quiet): "How do you handle calls when you're on a job?"

THE DEMO CLOSE: "Don't take my word for it — call this number when we hang up:
[DEMO NUMBER]. It answers like it's your front desk. If you'd hang up on it,
tell me Thursday and I'll leave you alone."

BOOKING CLOSE: "Ten-minute setup call and it's live on your line before we
hang up. Tuesday morning or Wednesday afternoon?"
