# Objection Bank (v1 — /coach updates from real calls via learnings.md)

"We already have someone answering."
-> "For after-hours too? And when she's on the other line? Those are the calls
I'm talking about — the ones nobody hears about because they never got answered."

"$500 is a lot." (or $199)
-> "What's an average [furnace repair/service call] worth? So one saved missed
call a month pays for it. And if it doesn't book you a single job in 30 days,
you don't pay — I'm carrying the risk, not you."

"AI will annoy my customers."
-> "Fair — I'd think that too. Call it right now: [demo number]. Thirty seconds.
If you'd hang up on it, tell me and I'm gone."

"Send me some info."
-> "I'll do one better — the info is a phone number. Call [demo number] after
we hang up, it IS the product. I'll ring you Thursday to hear your verdict."

"Need to talk to my wife/partner."
-> "Of course. What's the one thing they'll ask that you can't answer yet?
Let me give you that now. And have them call the demo line too — it's more
convincing than either of us."

"We don't miss that many calls."
-> (If audited): "I called you twice this week and no one picked up — that's
two in two tries." (If not): "Most owners think that. Want me to check? I'll
call your line after hours tonight and text you what happened."

"Bad timing / busy season."
-> "Busy season is exactly when you're missing the most calls — that's peak
leak. Setup is 10 minutes and I do it for you. Slow season is when you
DON'T need me."
