# Testimonial & Proof Ask — for the 3 active users (call, don't email)

Call script:
"Hey [name], Adam from Argora. Quick favor — I'm putting real customer results
on the site instead of marketing fluff. Three things, two minutes:
1. In your words, what's it actually done for you?
2. Can I pull one number — like how many after-hours calls it answered for
   you in June?
3. Can I use a short recording of it booking one of your calls on my site?
   I'll pick one with no customer details, and you approve it first."

Then: "One more — who's one other [trade] owner you know who's terrible at
answering his phone? I'll give you a free month if they sign up."

Log every answer verbatim in learnings.md — their words become ad copy,
landing page headlines, and LinkedIn posts.
