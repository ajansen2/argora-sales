# The Missed-Call Audit (signature outbound play)

Rules: max 2 test calls per business (one after-hours ~7pm, one mid-day ~11am).
If they answer, identify yourself: "Actually, you passed the test — I call
local [trade] companies to see who picks up. Most don't. Since I've got you..."
Log result in pipeline.csv hook column: "AUDIT: no answer 7pm Tue + 11am Thu".

Opener when unanswered (call or voicemail):
"Hi [name], Adam with Argora here in Edmonton. Straight up — I called your
line Tuesday at 7pm and Thursday at 11am. No answer either time. If I'd been
a customer with a burst pipe, I'm already calling your competitor. I run an
answering service built for [trade] — it picks up 24/7, books the job, texts
the caller. Hear it yourself: call [demo number]. If it wouldn't have saved
those calls, hang up on it and I'll leave you alone."

Email version (CASL footer required): same evidence, under 90 words,
demo number CTA.
